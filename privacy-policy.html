Overview

Zentrix Media collects limited business and contact information to reply to inquiries, schedule strategy calls, prepare proposals, and deliver services. This Privacy Policy applies to information submitted through this website and through direct contact routes linked from the website.

By using the site or contacting Zentrix through the site, you acknowledge the practices described on this page.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit a contact form, request a strategy call, or reach out directly, Zentrix may receive details such as your name, email address, company or brand name, website, market, business model, availability, preferred contact method, and any project or growth details you choose to share.

Technical and usage information

Like most websites, basic technical data may be collected automatically by hosting or infrastructure providers. This can include IP address, browser type, device information, referral source, timestamps, and pages viewed.

Communication records

If you contact Zentrix through email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or Instagram, the content of those communications and associated profile or contact details may be retained so the conversation can be managed properly.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and follow-up requests.
  • To review your current acquisition setup and prepare for a strategy call.
  • To send proposals, service information, or next-step recommendations.
  • To improve the site, service experience, and communication flow.
  • To protect the website, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Zentrix may also use submitted information to maintain internal records related to service discussions, onboarding, and project delivery.

Third-Party Tools and Limited Sharing

Zentrix only shares data when it is reasonably required to run the business or comply with the law. Examples include:

  • Form processing providers used to receive website inquiries and booking requests.
  • Email, messaging, scheduling, hosting, and infrastructure tools used to communicate and operate the site.
  • Social platforms, only when you choose to initiate contact through them.
  • Legal, regulatory, or security-related disclosures when required.

The website currently uses Formspree to process form submissions. If a live calendar provider is enabled later, booking requests may also pass through that provider.

Retention, International Use, and Your Choices

Zentrix is based in Pakistan and works remotely with businesses in other regions. Information may therefore be processed or stored by service providers located outside your country.

Inquiry and project-related information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for communication, service delivery, recordkeeping, and legal or operational purposes.

You can request access to the personal information Zentrix holds about you, request a correction, or ask for deletion, subject to any legal or contractual requirements that make limited retention necessary.

This website is intended for business use and is not knowingly directed to children.

Updates and Contact
